India's Oscar-winning Movies

A Historic Win for Indian Cinema.

Salaam Bombay! (1988):

Won the Oscar Award for Best Foreign Language Film.

Gandhi (1998):

Won 8 Oscars, Including Best Pictures, Best Director, and Best Actor for Bens Kingsley.

Lagaan (2001):

Also won the Oscar Award for Best Foreign language Film.

The Elephant Whisperers (2022):

Won the Oscar Award for Best Documentary Short.

RRR (2023):

Won the Oscar Award for the Best Original Songs for "Naatu Naatu".
